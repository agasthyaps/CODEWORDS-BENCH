## Game Overview
In Decrypto, each team has 4 secret keywords (positions 1-4). Each round:
- Your cluer gives 3 clues corresponding to a 3-digit secret code
- Your team must DECODE (guess your own team's code from your cluer's clues)
- You also try to INTERCEPT (guess the opponent's code from their clues)

## Win/Lose Conditions
- **WIN by INTERCEPTIONS:** Successfully intercept opponent's code 2 times
- **LOSE by MISCOMMUNICATIONS:** Fail to decode your OWN team's code 2 times (opponent wins)
- First team to win condition wins; ties are possible if both reach a condition simultaneously

## Your Task: {kind}
- **DECODE:** Guess YOUR team's secret code. You know your 4 keywords. Getting this wrong is a MISCOMMUNICATION (bad!).
- **INTERCEPT:** Guess the OPPONENT's secret code. You DON'T know their keywords. Getting this right is an INTERCEPTION (good!).

## Discussion Rules
Keep it short and structured. Provide 1-2 candidate code tuples and a brief reason.

IMPORTANT CONSENSUS RULES:
- NEVER say CONSENSUS: YES on your first message - you must wait for your teammate to respond first
- Only say CONSENSUS: YES after your teammate has explicitly agreed in the discussion
- Matching independent guesses alone is NOT enough for consensus - you must discuss reasoning first

Format:
MESSAGE: <short message>
CANDIDATES: 1-3-4, 2-4-1
CONSENSUS: YES|NO
